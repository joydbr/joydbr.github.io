Lv Healing Light
1 When the round starts, the connector with the lower HP% will restore 8% of the max HP, twice at most.
2 HP restored increased to 12%.
3 When the connector is healed for the first time, in the next rounds, 5% of the max HP will be restored when the round starts, once at most
4 HP of healing increased to 7,5%.
5 Restoring chances +2
6 Continous healing takes effect twice for each fighters, once at most every two rounds.

4 DMG will increase to 12%
5 conversion chances +1
6 Two marks can be added. If the mark is not removed it will exist forever
